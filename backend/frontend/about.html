About The Organization

Food Donation & Tracking is a platform that connects food donors with local organizations and charities to ensure surplus food reaches those in need. Our mission is to eliminate hunger and food waste by enabling a seamless donation process. Together, we can make a positive impact in our communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a seamless platform that connects generous food donors with local organizations and charities in need. By facilitating this connection, we strive to ensure surplus food reaches those who need it the most, providing nourishment and support to vulnerable individuals and families.
